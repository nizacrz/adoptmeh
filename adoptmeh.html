WHO WE ARE

adoptmeh is a non-profit organization that was founded by Team Cascaders, students of T.I.P Quezon City dedicated to promoting adoption and rescuing the voiceless animals that needs a forever home and a family that can give them a second chance in life.

Our services support voluntarily feeding the stray dogs and cats, up until organizing an event for stray animals to sustain the needs of the animals such as foods, medicine, and vets, which is funded by the adoptmeh company and supported by the people who are wholeheartedly willing to donate for the welfare of these abandoned animals.

WHY CHOOSE TO ADOPT?

YOU will help break the cycle of pet overpopulation. You help stop cruelty in mass breeding facilities. You pay less. You encourage others to adopt animals from shelters. When you adopt a pet, you are SAVING LIFE 🐾

Start your adoption journey today with adoptmeh!
