About Us

The company “adoptmeh.com” was founded by Team Cascaders, students of T.I.P Quezon City. Adoptmeh is a non-profit organization that is dedicated to promoting adoption and rescue to the voiceless animals that need a forever home and a family that can give them a second chance in life. The organization aims to create an adoption and rescue web system concerning animal welfare, animal care, animal control, and other animal-related concerns where people can browse and select the rescued pet they want to adopt before physically going to the shelter.
